Board Rules

  1. Images may be cleared automatically, so do not use this as storage. Please avoid posting NSFW images.
  2. No posting of illegal content. Images and comments should not offend the law.
  3. No personal attacks and harassment. Respect with others.
  4. Posting on topic. Postings should always be related to the topic of any posted image.
  5. Board appropriate. Images should be posted only in its proper category.
  6. Respect to other's privacy. Keep away from the spreading of personal info.
  7. Do not spam.
  8. All images are hosted on ImgBB. Make sure your uploads comply with their terms of service.
  9. All comments must be constructive and civil. Posting abusive language is not tolerated.
  10. Administrators retain the right to delete any post or comment they deem in violation of these rules.
  11. Anything deleted is password-protected. Don't forget your password!
